02    XP-Tech Technology

Built to Last
Generations.

XP-Tech composite panels combine a fiberglass-reinforced plastic surface with an XPS foam core — engineered to be tougher, lighter and more durable than traditional meranti or aluminium frame construction.

Lightweight Construction
Composite panels reduce overall weight, making the caravan easier to tow and compatible with a wider range of vehicles.
Waterproof & Rotproof
Seamless one-piece construction minimises water leak risk. No internal cavity rot — unlike timber-framed alternatives.
Superior Strength
CFRT skins with continuous fibre-reinforced tapes offer unparalleled impact damage tolerance and high surface strength.
High Thermal Insulation
XPS foam core delivers superior thermal efficiency — cooler in summer, warmer in winter, using less energy off-grid.
Noise Insulation
Significantly reduced sound transmission creates a quieter, more comfortable interior — ideal in caravan parks.
Anti-Corrosion
Complete anti-corrosion properties and electrical insulation — built to withstand Australia's harshest conditions.
