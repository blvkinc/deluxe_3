01    Overview

Bigger.
Bolder.

Introducing our biggest and most spacious 2-berth caravan. An impressive feature is the separate main bed and the rear lounge area that extends the full width of the caravan — ideal for a couple who likes to enjoy time with fellow caravanners or just wants the extra space. Equipped with the same luxuries as the Eclipse 21', with even more storage and a roomy layout complete with all the amenities you'd expect from a luxury RV.

  • Queen Island Bed with Lift-Up Storage
  • Rear Club Lounge spanning full width
  • Club Lounge with Drop-Down Table with TIC Cushion
  • Dometic Fresh Jet Air Conditioning
  • 3 × 170W Solar + 2 × 100Ah Lithium
  • Full Off-Road Independent 3.5T Suspension
  • 6" Dura-Gal Chassis + DO35 Coupling
  • 188L Dometic Compressor Fridge
