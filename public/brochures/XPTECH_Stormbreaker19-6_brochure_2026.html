XP-Tech Technology

Innovative.
Timeless.
Tough.

XP-Tech panels are advanced sandwich-structured composites combining a fiberglass-reinforced plastic surface with an XPS foam core. Unlike aluminium panels, they resist dents. Unlike wooden frames, they are moisture-resistant — no rot, no leaks. A caravan built for generations.

Lightweight
XP-Tech composite panels are designed to be lightweight — easier to tow, compatible with a wider range of vehicles, and more payload capacity for your belongings.
Waterproof & Rotproof
Built using seamless one-piece construction, minimising water leak risk. The ultimate solution for long-lasting protection against Australia's harsh conditions.
Noise Insulation
Superior acoustic properties significantly reduce sound transmission from inside and out — creating a quiet, relaxing atmosphere whether in a caravan park or the outback.
Thermal Insulation
Superior thermal efficiency helps maintain cooler interiors in summer, warmer in winter — reach your ideal temperature faster using less energy, especially off-grid.
Superior Strength
CFRT skins use thermoplastic properties laminated with continuous fiber-reinforced unidirectional tapes — unparalleled toughness, high durability, and exceptional impact damage tolerance.
Cost Effective
Lighter construction reduces fuel consumption for your tow vehicle — saving you money on every trip while minimising your environmental impact across Australia's roads.
