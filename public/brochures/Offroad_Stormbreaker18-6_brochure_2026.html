Overview

Compact.
Capable.
Complete.

The Stormbreaker 18'6" is our smallest family caravan — and it punches well above its weight. Engineered for families who refuse to compromise, this caravan effortlessly combines snug comfort with stylish design and genuine off-road durability. Whether you're navigating corrugated outback roads or pulling into a coastal campsite, the Stormbreaker is built for the journey.

Queen Bed — East-West Orientation
2 or 3 Bunks — 1.9m Each, with Windows
6" Dura-Gal Chassis + TuffRide 3.0T Suspension
Sleeps 5 — Ideal for the Whole Family

The east-west bed orientation is the clever space-saving secret that lets this compact 18'6" van accommodate queen sleeping, two full bunks, and a split bathroom — all without feeling squeezed.

Key Features

Built for Families.
Built for the Road.

01
Smart Sleeping Layout
Queen east-west bed for the parents, plus two or three spacious 1.9m bunks with windows for the kids. Sleeps five comfortably in a compact footprint.
02
Offroad Engineered
6" Dura-Gal 2" Raiser chassis, TuffRide 3.0T suspension, 16" alloy wheels with heavy-duty offroad tyres, 12" electric brakes, and checkerplate protection front and sides.
03
Solar Power System
Three 170W solar panels keep you charged off-grid. Backed by 2 × 100Ah lithium batteries, twin 95L water tanks, and a 110L grey water tank.
04
188L Dometic Fridge
A full-size 188L Dometic compressor fridge keeps your family's supplies fresh, paired with a laminate benchtop, Mini Grill, 3-burner gas stove & 1 electric burner, and microwave.
05
Split Bathroom
Separate shower and toilet for practicality on the road. ABS lightweight shower, Thetford toilet, and an external shower for rinsing off after adventures.
06
Modern Electrical
LED lighting throughout, USB charging points built into every reading light, Dometic Fresh Jet air conditioning, reverse camera, and front/rear LED light bars.
