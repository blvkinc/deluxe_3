XP-Tech Technology

Innovative.
Timeless.
Tough.

XP-Tech panels are advanced sandwich-structured composites combining a fiberglass-reinforced plastic surface with an XPS foam core. No rot, no leaks, no dents. A caravan built for generations.

Lightweight
Designed to be lightweight — easier to tow, compatible with a wider range of vehicles, and more payload capacity for your belongings.
Waterproof & Rotproof
Seamless one-piece construction minimises water leak risk. The ultimate solution for long-lasting protection against Australia's harsh conditions.
Noise Insulation
Superior acoustic properties significantly reduce sound transmission — creating a quiet, relaxing atmosphere wherever you park.
Thermal Insulation
Superior thermal efficiency — cooler in summer, warmer in winter. Reach your ideal temperature faster using less energy, especially off-grid.
Superior Strength
CFRT skins use thermoplastic properties laminated with continuous fiber-reinforced unidirectional tapes — unparalleled toughness and impact tolerance.
Cost Effective
Lighter construction reduces fuel consumption for your tow vehicle — saving you money and minimising your environmental impact on every trip.
