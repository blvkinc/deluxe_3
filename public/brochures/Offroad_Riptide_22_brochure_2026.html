Overview

Built for
Every Road.

The Riptide 22' offers the perfect balance of comfort and functionality for your family adventures. This model combines spacious living areas with practical features, making it an ideal choice for families seeking a reliable and comfortable caravan. With its well-thought-out layout and premium finishes, the Riptide 22' provides all the amenities you need for memorable journeys.

Key Features

Ready for
Anything.

01
Smart Layout
Queen island bed with lift-up storage for the parents, two spacious 2.0m bunks for the kids, and an L-Shape lounge with TIC cushion — all in a practical 22' package. Sleeps four comfortably.
02
Truss Flat Chassis
6" Dura-Gal Truss Flat Chassis with 6" Raiser, dual axle, Full Offroad Independent 3.5T suspension, 16" alloy wheels with heavy-duty offroad tyres, and 12" electric brake system.
03
Solar Power System
Three 170W solar panels keep you powered off-grid. Backed by 2×100Ah lithium batteries, 2×95L fresh water tanks, and a 110L grey water tank.
04
188L Dometic Fridge
Full-size 188L Dometic compressor fridge, premium laminate benchtop, Oven (3 x Gas | 1 x Electric Burner and Grill), and 25L microwave for effortless meal prep.
05
Split Bathroom
ABS lightweight shower and Thetford toilet. External shower for rinsing off after adventures, premium black taps, and a water filter for quality drinking water.
06
Modern Electrical
Projecta 30A charger + 30A MPPT, LED lighting throughout, USB charging in every bunk and bedside, Dometic Series 7 air conditioning, reverse camera, front/rear LED light bars.
