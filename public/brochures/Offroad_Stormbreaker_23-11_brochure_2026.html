Overview

Compact.
Capable.
Complete.

Got more than 3 kids or always wanted a caravan with a spacious living space for the whole family? Look no further than the Stormbreaker 23'11". A key feature that sets this model apart is the large, separate ensuite at the rear. Built with premium interior, exterior, and mechanical features — when you hit the open road with your loved ones, you'll enjoy an exceptionally roomy layout with all the amenities of a luxury RV.

Queen or King Island Bed Orientation
2 to 6 Bunks — 1.9m Each
6" Dura-Gal Chassis + Full Offroad Independent 3.5T Suspension
Sleeps Upto 8 — Ideal for the Whole Family

The island bed is the clever space-saving secret that lets this compact 23'11" van accommodate queen or king sleeping, two to six full bunks, and a separate ensuite — all without feeling squeezed.

Key Features

Built for Families.
Built for the Road.

01
Smart Sleeping Layout
Queen or king island bed for the parents, plus two to six spacious 1.9m bunks for the kids. Sleeps upto eight comfortably in a spacious footprint.
02
Offroad Engineered
6" Dura-Gal 2" Raiser chassis, Full Offroad Independent 3.5T suspension, 16" alloy wheels with heavy-duty offroad tyres, 12" electric brake system, and checkerplate protection front, back and sides.
03
Solar Power System
Three 200W solar panels keep you charged off-grid. Backed by 2 × 100Ah lithium batteries, twin 110L water tanks, and a 110L grey water tank.
04
188L Dometic Fridge
A full-size 188L Dometic compressor fridge keeps your family's supplies fresh, paired with a premium laminate benchtop, Oven (3 x Gas | 1 x Electric Burner and Grill), and 25L microwave.
05
Separate Ensuite
Separate shower and toilet for practicality on the road. ABS lightweight shower, Thetford toilet, and an external shower for rinsing off after adventures.
06
Modern Electrical
LED lighting throughout, USB charging points built into every reading light, Dometic Fresh Jet air conditioning, washing machine, reverse camera, and front/rear LED light bars.
